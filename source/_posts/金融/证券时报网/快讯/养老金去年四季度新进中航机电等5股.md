<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，截止3月18日，2021年四季度末养老金账户共现身6只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进5只。持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300772" code="300772">运达股份</a>，持股量为317.18万股，占流通股比例1.65%。</p><p>持股数量方面，去年年末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" code="002013">中航机电</a>的持股量最多，持股量为2751.86万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601016" code="601016">节能风电</a>，持股量为751.04万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在1亿元以上的有3只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" code="002013">中航机电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300390" code="300390">天华超净</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300772" code="300772">运达股份</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220318364628499509.png" title="四季度养老金持股明细.png" alt="四季度养老金持股明细.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020220318364628499509.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
