<p style="text-align: left;">证券时报网讯，据绿地集团消息，7月15日，绿地集团与<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600918" code="600918">中泰证券</a>签署战略合作协议，双方将在金融合作等方面建立长期稳定的战略合作关系，同时<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600918" code="600918">中泰证券</a>将落户绿地外滩中心最后2栋总部办公楼。截至目前，绿地外滩中心已接连迎来<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601319" code="601319">中国人保</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601229" code="601229">上海银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000750" code="000750">国海证券</a>、建信人寿等多家重量级金融企业，随着<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600918" code="600918">中泰证券</a>的正式落户，7栋总部办公已全部被抢滩登陆。</p>
