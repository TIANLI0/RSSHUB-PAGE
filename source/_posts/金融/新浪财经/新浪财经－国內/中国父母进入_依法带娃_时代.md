<p>　　原标题：中国父母进入“依法带娃”时代</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　新年开新局，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1月1日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《中华人民共和国家庭教育促进法》</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正式实施。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也就是说，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">所有家长必须“依法带娃”了。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/580/w690h690/20220104/3414-84c41ad8e720a293951131c3a5a009ff.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">从“家事”到“国事”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《中华人民共和国家庭教育促进法》</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是继《教育法》、《义务教育法》</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和新版《未成年人保护法》后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　又一部教育领域的重磅法规，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也是我国首次就家庭教育进行专门立法。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　家庭教育是教育的开端，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　关乎未成年人的健康成长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和家庭的幸福安宁，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但家庭教育具有私密性和个性化的特点，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当“家事”上升到“国事”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　该如何尊重家庭教育的自身规律？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　立法的意图，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就是为“促进”家庭教育</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而进行的“指引”和“赋能”。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/179/w668h311/20220104/3f7e-6dfa3e4c994ee7c6d327b030a940c3e3.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　指引，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">是通过法律的方式告诉父母，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">如何当好一个合格的家长，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引导家长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　按照科学的方法和理念教育孩子。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　赋能，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是指家长遇到困难和问题时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可以向公共体系获取相应的帮助，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　学习到相关的知识，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然后来提升自己的教育能力。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/215/w668h347/20220104/c66f-3e69cf7241867ee69cafb64629923f02.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/697/w666h31/20220104/f25d-55e2973067278ee20f6df7168550d546.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">相关部门如何介入？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　既然立法是为“促进”家庭教育</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而进行的“指引”和“赋能”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于拒不履行家庭教育责任的家长来说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更多是纠偏，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　帮助家长更好地开展家庭教育。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正因如此，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　该法在后续审议过程中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　删除了一审稿中的罚款、拘留等惩罚措施，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">改为对家长的批评教育、劝诫制止、</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">予以训诫等措施。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/690/w412h278/20220104/7429-c4cbff429d3cbc3c2521d9637a4b70ec.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在四川泸州叙永县的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全国首个涉诉未成年人家庭教育指导工作站，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　刘某和张某夫妻俩正在接受家庭教育指导。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　双方的离婚纠纷，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　导致13岁的女儿产生厌学情绪，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不间断逃学，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但刘某和张某却放任不管，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　老师多次家访劝女孩返校也无效。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　于是，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　承办法官向这对夫妻发出</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“责令接受家庭教育指导令”，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">指派两名家庭教育指导师对其进行指导。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/216/w667h349/20220104/ac7c-e7cc3b7211f058182ac2074d7ad79acc.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2021年11月，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">全国法院系统首个设立的</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">涉诉未成年人家教指导工作站</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在叙永县法院少审庭挂牌成立。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由叙永县团委、妇联、教体局等联合推荐的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　14名家教指导员受聘领证上岗，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们分别是法官、学校心理咨询老师、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　专业社工以及民政、妇联</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和关工委等部门的工作人员，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这张由家庭、学校、社会、司法四位一体</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为未成年人撑起的“保护网”开始发挥作用。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/217/w670h347/20220104/cadd-b53dd5ad10c21ca2deb6ecf87a463963.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/702/w671h31/20220104/baf8-17d44b88ec2ea0a3be5b33bf0e8326fe.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/698/w669h29/20220104/f621-ac272afe6e89938da2af6a10cf86d02c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">新法与“双减”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　新实施的《家庭教育促进法》的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　另一大亮点是<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">强调立德树人。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而要想发挥家庭教育</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在孩子品德教育</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和人格养成方面的优势，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有一个问题无法回避，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那就是“双减”。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/218/w671h347/20220104/5f9f-12129f2d768ecac684473e5f335df954.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2021年7月，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中办、国办印发</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《关于进一步减轻义务教育阶段学生作业负担和校外培训负担的意见》，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在“双减”背景下</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　出台的《家庭教育促进法》</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自然肩负起落实“双减”的重任。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《家庭教育促进法》规定：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/161/w670h291/20220104/3858-f57cad0cc3828f25ea17681ceee52ca7.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　严管校外培训机构，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　提升学校教学质量，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都有助于推进“双减”政策，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但从某种意义上讲，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　家长才是真正让政策落地的关键。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　专家表示，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“双减”和《家庭教育促进法》的宗旨一致，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都是为了立德树人。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这样的背景下，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　学校和家庭应协同确立孩子的教育价值观，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　家庭教育要配合学校，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　减少“短视化”、“功利化”的目标，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把重心放在“育人”上；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　学校也要对家长进行正确引导。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/218/w671h347/20220104/ea2b-3fb9ad002b5ec48d76b5cc97d029791c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
