<strong>居家饲养宠物应该注意哪些安全隐患？</strong><br>小爪溜达的回答<br><p></p><p data-pid="cnWrvCOX">以下回答以<b>家中无人的情况为前提，</b>家中有人的时候，大多数会及时制止或处理危险的发生。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><h2>一、笼养</h2><p data-pid="wMmTEQlk">不管猫咪还是狗狗或其他小宠（如兔子、仓鼠等），如果是笼养的话，需要注意以下几点：</p><p data-pid="5Lx_HW87">1）<b>笼子最好放在地面，不要放在高处</b>，以免宠物冲撞笼子，导致笼子从高处坠落，摔伤宠物。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-e7d2f0ed89b4c66a21c59938124de916_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="2269" data-rawheight="3393" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4944d6c23945ecd182a2869d6df6082d_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-e7d2f0ed89b4c66a21c59938124de916_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="B8X6rqXl">2）<b>笼子内或者笼子周边不要放置宠物容易误食的物品</b>，如纸巾、塑料袋、纸箱等物品。避免宠物误食引起的危险。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-e64e13cf7ae7c6002b163b10ad70f1be_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1439" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-01f2df09c394a47d5aff8a0f688ebc1c_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-e64e13cf7ae7c6002b163b10ad70f1be_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="jry2610H">3）<b>不要将笼子放在阳台等太阳直射的地方，防止宠物中暑</b>。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-db83a3c24d0446386a8b569651cd9948_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="771" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-076955bc84b08971500538dd31977e4c_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-db83a3c24d0446386a8b569651cd9948_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="Gv1NW2zL">4）<b>将笼子放置在干燥环境，避免宠物因潮气过重导致湿疹等皮肤病</b>。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-906d73661964cce48f993829c06f9842_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="450" data-rawheight="313" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-bb575266495439ad8d8c2c3e2daa5fc2_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-906d73661964cce48f993829c06f9842_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><h2>二、非笼养</h2><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="edppjRS7"><b>1）误食风险</b></p><ul><li data-pid="1GDyqwv5">将<b>塑料袋、塑料瓶</b>等易吞食的物品收起，尽量放在宠物够不到的地方，<b>避免误食造成窒息、划伤肠道的风险。</b></li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8097ff413f2cc041f4b05b96a22d52d9_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="761" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5a611e20e99c2bb603921ece30c5586a_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8097ff413f2cc041f4b05b96a22d52d9_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><ul><li data-pid="sa4nummI"><b>洗衣液、消毒液等化学物品收起来</b>，尽量放在宠物不去或者够不到的地方，避免误食。</li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-70d3e0f5c4d8a0c364e63fe8f39216ec_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="720" data-rawheight="480" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d78ef5ee3c27e1b32b07c3a3d1030b5a_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-70d3e0f5c4d8a0c364e63fe8f39216ec_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><ul><li data-pid="oxQ7CGB-"><b>家中有毒的植物应远离宠物放置</b>，如百合、绣球、郁金香、滴水观音、龟背竹等常见的家养植物。</li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-a424823740cf2ee4fd3d48a9291afa99_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="2730" data-rawheight="4096" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-7c06b0647c078608e42ecf9b7ce251c5_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-pid="t4UrRNUQ"><b>及时清理垃圾桶中的垃圾</b>，避免宠物翻垃圾桶进而误食。</li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f300e6cab25ca8b6d0f72cbf91db95e3_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="641" data-rawheight="436" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-f9ac6f21f92a5e03281f0755858b692d_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f300e6cab25ca8b6d0f72cbf91db95e3_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><ul><li data-pid="L3cEerUb"><b>宠物不能吃的食物要放在宠物够不到的地方</b>，例如巧克力、葡萄、夏威夷果、各种药品等。</li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c88155dabca5db0aaa0cb2e50460370e_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="636" data-rawheight="451" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1f134e4773be2d16d43b18d934dac333_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c88155dabca5db0aaa0cb2e50460370e_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="2WchCqTN"><b>2）水电火等危险物品</b></p><ul><li data-pid="8I8ELsz3">出门前，将能关的电源关闭，能拔下来的插头拔下，最好将电线收起，尽量不要露出。<b>防止宠物啃咬电线发生触电风险。</b></li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-13de4bd82f3176649fa6cbd01ec07b14_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="750" data-rawheight="500" data-pid="kgwY6R9T"><b>打火机要及时收起来</b>，避免宠物啃咬发生爆炸或误食。</li></ul><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-47f3483aad592e9ce8907425473b27ee_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="395" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a6430371b410d2af48e12589c8fd0636_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-pid="pC1aqsX7">对于会开水龙头的猫咪，最好出门时，<b>将有水龙头的房间门关起来</b>。免得回家发现“水漫金山”。</li></ul><figure data-size="small"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ceab7966e32a85c23d1341488f4573b4_1440w.jpg" data-caption
