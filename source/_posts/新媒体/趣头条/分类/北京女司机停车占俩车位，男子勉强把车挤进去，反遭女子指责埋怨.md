src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/images/2021/12/25/1640403937211318442.jpg" data-size="1079,1172" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>根据曝光的现场视频显示，卡罗拉的左侧车轮已经进入了左侧的车位里，也就是说卡罗拉一辆车占了两个车位，而男网友的车是紧贴着卡罗拉停在了车位里。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/images/2021/12/25/1640403937497615364.jpg" data-size="1080,587" alt="2.jpg" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/images/2021/12/25/1640403937766920473.jpg" data-size="1080,587" alt="3.jpg"
