<p>近日，四川资阳。在一次执法检查中，执法人员现场扣押了“冰墩墩”侵权T恤13件，对涉事服装店经营者进行了普法教育，并依法予以立案调查。相关部门提醒广大市民：未经奥林匹克标志权利人许可，任何单位或个人都不得以商业目的使用奥林匹克标志。同时请市民朋友增强奥林匹克标志知识产权保护意识，通过官方渠道购买相关产品，拒绝购买侵权伪劣商品。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/9bc3dc0b2260b458f5c5a03ba0a57061.jpeg" data-size="440,212" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/0923706e0879e50eee562afa87e4ba43.jpeg" data-size="440,181" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/ebad503b465153f3a1eb8e16f3b3b30c.jpeg" data-size="440,211" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/a31c3ab7cf815bd0a4bc2988650717d3.jpeg" data-size="440,149" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/13b6c40f42b4be23ff353a964c7b1e1e.jpeg" data-size="440,212" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/e998b0b87e78d512a7a2a9e6b6784027.jpeg" data-size="440,211" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/73ce8c0d9fa32a007a010f73be439271.jpeg" data-size="440,213" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/2ebe584544443e01ed4206392574901d.jpeg" data-size="440,213" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/15/fe960697dd9162cf31ba652cdbccca96.jpeg" data-size="440,210" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
