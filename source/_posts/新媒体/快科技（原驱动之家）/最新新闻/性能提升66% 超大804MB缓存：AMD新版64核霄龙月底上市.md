<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>去年底推出了新版的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC 7003</span>系列霄龙处理器，代号为<span lang="EN-US">Milan-X</span>，依然是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>架构，最多<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核，但用上了<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>缓存，总容量高达<span lang="EN-US">804MB</span>，性能提升多达<span lang="EN-US">66%</span>，现在该系列处理器将在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月份上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>高级副总裁兼服务器业务部总经理<span lang="EN-US">Dan McNamara</span>在采访中确认了此事，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>称代号为<span lang="EN-US">Milan-X </span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU </span>正在向客户提供样品，预计将在本月底推出该产品。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到之前有爆料称消费级的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>处理器也是本月底前上市，这意味着<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>处理器终于能全面上市了。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="性能提升66% 超大804MB缓存：AMD新版64核霄龙月底上市" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220304/a14dfd41-26da-46e1-80da-e13f3d406896.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Milan-X</span>处理器包括四款型号，<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核心的霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7773X</span>、<span lang="EN-US">32</span>核心的霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7573X</span>、<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核心的霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7473X</span>、<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核心的霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7373X</span>，三级缓存从原有的最多<span lang="EN-US">256MB</span>统一增加到<span lang="EN-US">768MB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>如果再算上<span lang="EN-US">4MB</span>一级缓存、<span lang="EN-US">32MB</span>二级缓存，一颗<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核心的霄龙，就拥有恐怖的<span lang="EN-US">804MB</span>缓存，双路就是几乎<span lang="EN-US">1.6GB</span>！</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于性能提升，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>表示，更多的缓存可以大大减轻系统内存带宽压力，<strong>同样<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">Milan-X</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Milan</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Synopsys VCS</span>上实测<span lang="EN-US">EDA RTL</span>验证工作负载，性能提升了多达<span lang="EN-US">66</span>％。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7003X</span>系列已经赢得了微软、戴尔、联想、惠与、超微、思科等众多客户的新方案。</p>
