<p class="MsoNormal">也许是今年实在太缺芯片了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/767/767201.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>前不久发布了一款很独特的桌面系统——<span lang="EN-US">4700S</span></a>，集成了主板、处理器、内存、散热器，这应该是<span lang="EN-US">PS5</span>主机上的废芯片做成的准系统。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4700S</span>系统的规格看着还不错，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>核心，搭配<span lang="EN-US">8GB GDDR6</span>做内存<span lang="EN-US">/</span>显存，芯片组是<span lang="EN-US">A77E</span>，</strong></span>但本该最期待的核显被屏蔽了，不然就是完整的<span lang="EN-US">PS5</span>硬件系统了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">为了平衡，<span lang="EN-US">4700S</span>系统搭配的是<span lang="EN-US">Radeon 550</span>独显，基于<span lang="EN-US">14nm Polaris 12</span>核心，<span lang="EN-US">512</span>个流处理器，<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位显存位宽。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="PS5芯片废物利用 AMD称已交付80多款4700S系统：8核Zen2" h="683" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210712/a52d7c94-0281-42b3-9cfd-21417c0ee6ec.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="PS5芯片废物利用 AMD称已交付80多款4700S系统：8核Zen2" h="496" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210712/a3a2621e-d236-410e-9288-4e2c51e589f4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">整套<span lang="EN-US">4700S</span>的性能测试下来，<strong><span lang="EN-US">3.2GHz</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能不错，内存带宽比普通<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>还要快，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>性能则要明显好于<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">UHD 750</span>核显。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这样一套硬件系统怎么看都有点奇葩，与主机、游戏<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>都格格不入，但是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>表示这套<span lang="EN-US">4700S</span>桌面系统是给那些对性能、多核心数有要求的市场而生的，非常适合多任务处理、生产力及轻型<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>工作站。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还透露已经有<span lang="EN-US">80</span>多套系统基于<span lang="EN-US">4700S</span>了，</strong></span>这个还是相当让人意外的，没想到如此受欢迎。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于这样的准系统的售价，由于搭配的电源、<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>甚至显卡的不同，价格是不固定的，有<span lang="EN-US">300</span>多美元的，也有<span lang="EN-US">700</span>多美元的，具体取决于配置情况。</p>
