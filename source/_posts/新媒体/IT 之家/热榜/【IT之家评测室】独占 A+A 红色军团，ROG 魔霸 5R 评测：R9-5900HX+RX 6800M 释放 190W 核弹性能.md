显存的大量热量。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/7/e452c99f-85be-407e-9870-456ff82b4fd2.png" w="830" h="623" title="【IT之家评测室】独占 A+A 红色军团，ROG 魔霸 5R 评测：R9-5900HX+RX 6800M 释放 190W
